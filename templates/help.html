How do I start a recording?

Navigate to the "Prescriptions" page. Click the "Start Recording" button at the top of the page to begin capturing the conversation. Your microphone must be enabled in the browser.

Where is the transcribed text saved?

The transcribed text appears in real-time in the "Transcribed Text" box on the right-hand card of the "Prescriptions" page. Currently, the text is not saved automatically, but you can copy it or use the "Download Text" button if available.

How does the AI suggestion work?

The AI model analyzes the keywords and context from the transcribed conversation text to suggest relevant medicines. This feature is currently in development and will be enhanced in future updates.
